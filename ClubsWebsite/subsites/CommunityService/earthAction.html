🌎Every Tuesday, Earth Action’s club members pick up the recycling around Dover High! Join us in room 242 each Tuesday to help Earth Action keep the school clean & green!

A post shared by DHS Earth Action (@dhs.earthaction) on Apr 30, 2019 at 1:50pm PDT


Earth Action is a student-run environmental action club with a focus on moving Dover High School toward more environmentally sustainable practices. The club is hosted by Mr. Seekamp, Ms. Dromey-Heter and Ms. McCarron-Stewart. The club can be joined my anyone at any time!

Mettings: Mondays after school twice a month in room 302.
