Red Cross Club Teacher: Mr. LeClair Registration: Have to ask Mr. LeClair to join, then have to register nationally for Red Cross Society Meetings: Room 237 What we do: We are a community volunteer organization that helps with medical and environmental disasters, while promoting health education. Info: We started in 2017 and have been nationally registered every year since. How to find out about meetings: As a general pattern, we try to meet twice monthly on the first and third Thursdays of the month. You can also get meeting times from the morning announcements and other club members. Extra info: Every year, one or two of our students can win a scholarship for a varying amount every year.
