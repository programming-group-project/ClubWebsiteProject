Regular meetings each Monday from 5:00-7:00pm
Where?
McConnell Center in downtown Dover, ground floor, Door #7
Can anyone join?
Yes, space permitting. Grades 9-12. $120 participation fee and completed application required.
Other Information:
All activities promote a drug-free lifestyle. Students actively plan and conduct activities to reduce the harm of addiction and drug use. Students create radio ads that play on local stations; create presentations to perform in schools all over New England; perform skits; propose changes in state or local laws; and hold community events to get their message out. They also plan recreational events for members of Y-to-Y, just to have fun.
