Football Team Teacher: k.osbor@dover.k12.nh.usCoach Kenneth Osbor's Requirements to join: There are no requirements other than registering on family ID and getting a physical Fees: $75.00 for the school mandated transportation fee for all athletes Funding: We run fundraisers with the Boosters and the Coaches Meetings: See "How to find out about meetings" What we do: Varsity Football – JV Football – Reserve Football – Contact football played against other Division I teams around the state. Varsity games played on Friday night with the occasional Saturday game. JV game on Mondays and Reserve games on Thursday. Info: Football started at Dover High School,around 1906. How to find out about meetings: Students listen to announcements for a sign up meeting in June – addition morning announcements inviting anyone to join the team for off-season conditioning starting in December running through August, 8th grade info night, AD calls feeding schools and asks to post a sign up sheet in June. Extra info: The sport runs from August 13th through the third week of November if you make the playoffs and there is a commitment 6 days a week. Nothing on Sunday. We have one organizational meeting during the month of June, where we take sign-ups.
