Meetings:
The tennis team meets 1.5-2 hours, 6 days a week.

Info:
The past two years the boy tennis team qualified two groups for the doubles and two guys for the singles NH state tournament. Both doubles teams advanced. This past year Dover did the same with all individuals advancing the first round and one doubles team making it to the quarterfinals of the double tournament. This past year Dover finished the season ranked 4th in the state and hosted Concord for the first round of the NH team tournament. This was the first time that Dover boys qualified for the state team tournament since 2003. In the past few year several tennis coaches are offering tennis clinics with the goal of making the Dover tennis more competitive in the state.

Extra info:
NH High School tennis format is 6 singles and 3 doubles. All matches play an eight game pro set. Each match is awarded 1 point to the winner and the team that scores 5 points or more points is the winner.
